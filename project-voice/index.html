Project Voice

The Voice Project is a multi-semester project that aims to develop and assist alternative communication methods for nonverbal patients.

2026 Colloquium for the Improvement of Nonverbal Patient Care Technologies (CINPCT)

The goal of the colloquium is to bring awareness among academia, industry and students about how various existing technologies can come together to create assistive devices for nonverbal people. It is a mostly student-run conference, with additional assistance from many of the RPI faculty & administration.

Research

The research branch of Project Voice focuses on creating a home-use assistive communication device for nonspeaking individuals.
