Atomia - The Adventures of Arpie

Atomia is a 2D adventure role-playing game made for educational and entertainment purposes.

Follow Arpie and the ragtag elemental team as they make their way from the Outer Outskirts of Atomia to the Central Kingdom, where the Noble Gases are locked up by the Synthetic Syndicate. Can the team succesfully save the land from the evil clutches of the Syndicate, or were they doomed from the beginning?
