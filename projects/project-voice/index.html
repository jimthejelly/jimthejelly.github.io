Project Voice

The Voice Project was a multi-semester project that aimed to develop and assist alternative communication methods for nonverbal patients. This started as a project pitched by Ed Fennell to the Rensselaer Center for Open Source (RCOS), which I then helped take upon a leader role in helping push this cause forward.

2025 Colloquium for the Improvement of Nonverbal Patient Care Technologies (CINPCT)

This colloquium was aimed at bringing awareness among academia, industry and students about how various existing technologies can come together to create assistive devices for nonverbal people. It was a mostly student-run conference, with the assistance of Dr. Juergen Hahn and Ed Fennell.
